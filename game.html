How to Play

  • Click "Start Game" to start the game and to cast the first roll for round 1
  • Click "Roll Dice" button for round number 2
  • Click "Roll Dice" button for round number 3
  • After 3 rounds the winner is announced
  • The "Reset Game" button has to be pushed to reset the game
  • Alternately, the "Reset Game" button can be clicked at any time to rest the game

Scoring

  • Rolling a "1" or double "1" on the Dice will give an immediate 0 score for the round
  • Rolling any number on the dice will give you a score of the sum of those two number
  • Rolling doubles will give you s bonus for 2x the score for that round
